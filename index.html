Field Trip

On December 8th, I went on a field trip with the Web Design and Web Development to an Advertisement Agency know as Archer Malmo. The Day Started out with a normal first and second period. Then we got on the yellow bus and the drive was okay until we found out we had a flat tire. We were dropped off a little ways down from the building Archer Malmo was in.

Impressions and The Presentation

The building lobby was beautiful. When I first saw the fourth floor work space, I was surpised to see there was space and everyone on that floor had their own office. I like the fact there was a ping pong table too. When we were all together, we sat down in open space and for two hours, they presented and talk about the work field, themselves, coding, and Archer Malmo. It was very informative and it caught my attention at least.
